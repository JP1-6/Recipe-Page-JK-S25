Cozy Grilled Cheese

This warm and crispy grilled cheese sandwich is the perfect comfort food. With melted cheese and golden buttery bread, it's quick to make and always hits the spot.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Optional: a pinch of garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice evenly.
  2. Place a slice of cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and place the sandwich in the pan.
